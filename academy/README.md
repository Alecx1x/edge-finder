# Money Lab Academy

_The best real resources for every facet of the markets — vetted, not hyped._

> Educational only — not financial, legal, or tax advice. Every link was verified, but platforms and laws (especially prediction markets) change fast and vary by state. Nothing here sells picks or guaranteed profit; be skeptical of anything that does.

## Courses

- 🧮 **[Course 0 — Foundations](00-foundations.md)** — Probability, odds formats, expected value, variance, and the vig. The math under every tool in Money Lab. Start here even if you've bet for years.
- 📊 **[Course 1 — How Betting Markets Work](01-markets.md)** — Line-setting, sharp vs. square money, market-making, line movement, Closing Line Value, de-vigging, and how books limit/ban winners.
- 🎯 **[Course 2 — Finding Edges](02-edges.md)** — +EV value betting, arbitrage, middling, matched betting/promos, and DFS pick'em math (PrizePicks / Underdog / DK Pick6).
- 💰 **[Course 3 — Bankroll & Risk](03-bankroll.md)** — Kelly & fractional Kelly, risk of ruin, bet sizing, record-keeping, and why CLV beats short-term ROI as proof of skill.
- 🔮 **[Course 4 — Prediction Markets & Forecasting](04-prediction.md)** — Kalshi & Polymarket, event contracts, CFTC regulation, cross-market arbitrage, and forecasting as a trainable skill. The fastest-moving frontier.
- 📈 **[Course 5 — Stocks & Options](05-stocks.md)** — Market foundations, the Greeks, implied volatility, probability of profit, and the strategies in your calculator — from neutral, no-sales-pitch sources.
- 🪙 **[Course 6 — Crypto (Defense-First)](06-crypto.md)** — Tokens, DEXs, liquidity, on-chain analysis, and — most importantly — how to NOT get rugged. The single best filter: ask who profits from the 'advice.'
- 🔬 **[Course 7 — Quant & Modeling](07-quant.md)** — Backtesting, overfitting, calibration (Brier/log-loss), and rating systems (Elo/Glicko). How to know whether a model is real or just curve-fit.
- 🧠 **[Course 8 — Psychology & Discipline](08-discipline.md)** — Process over outcome, tilt, and the cognitive biases that quietly wreck good bettors and traders. The least technical course and arguably the most important.
- ⚖️ **[Course 9 — Legal, Tax & Responsible Play](09-legal.md)** — How winnings are taxed, the standard-deduction trap, state-by-state legality, and responsible-gambling resources. Boring, until it isn't.
